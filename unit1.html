Unit 1 - Online World

This unit explores how online services and internet technologies work.

Evidence

Online World helps learners understand how the internet and online technologies work and how they are used in everyday life. In this unit, learners explore topics such as computer networks, internet infrastructure, online communication, website hosting, cloud services, cybersecurity, and how data is transferred across networks. It develops knowledge of how devices connect and communicate safely and efficiently online. Through studying this unit, learners learn important digital and technical skills including understanding online systems, identifying security risks, using online services responsibly, and recognising how businesses and individuals rely on internet technologies. Learners also gain knowledge about legal and ethical issues such as data protection and online safety. This unit can support progression into future careers within the technology industry, including web development, cybersecurity, network engineering, IT support, software development, cloud computing, systems administration, and digital marketing. It also provides a strong foundation for further study in Information Technology and Computer Science.

Reflection

This unit was a great introduction to the world of online services and internet technologies. I learned about the different types of online services, such as social media, e-commerce, and cloud computing. I also gained an understanding of how the internet works, including the role of servers, clients, and protocols. I found the coursework to be engaging and informative, and it helped me develop a solid foundation in the online world. Overall, I am excited to continue exploring the digital landscape and applying what I have learned in future units.
